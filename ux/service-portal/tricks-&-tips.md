## Service Portal Tips and Tricks

This section collects **practical tips, diagnostic techniques, and implementation insights** that help you better understand how Service Portal behaves under the hood. The focus is on real‑world scenarios: identifying bottlenecks, validating assumptions, and making informed improvements rather than relying on trial and error.
