Inbound emails is one of the first ways folks can interact with ServiceNow from outside of ServiceNow 

Emails are handled through a system called inbound actionsinbound actions allow you to handle replies forwards and new messages differentlybut one thing they all share in common is that they have to find a user or guestand they will use key value pairs with the colon separating the field from the value on the right hand side

A few important bits about inbound actions is how to stop others from running and debugging them
